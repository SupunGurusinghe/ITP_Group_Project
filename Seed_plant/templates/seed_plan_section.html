SEED AND PLANT SECTION

In 2001 a consortium of partners were brought together to form a project management board. The aim of the board was to oversee a scientific research programme which examined the potential for biological or natural control of Japanese knotweed in Great Britain. CABI is a science-based not-for-profit organization that specialises in agricultural and environmental research. Its mission and direction is influenced by its 45 member countries who help guide the organisation's activities. These include scientific publishing, projects and consultancy, information for development and mycological services.
